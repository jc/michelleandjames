Michelle and James are Getting Married

24 September 2011 • The Allerton Hotel, Chicago, Illinois, USA.

Two good eggs found each other in Champaign, Illinois, USA. August 2008.

Arrival Information

Transportation is easy and abundant from O'Hare International to The Allerton. These are some of the options:

  • Follow Google's directions.
  • Alternatively, take the Blue line from O'Hare airport into downtown Chicago getting off at Monroe, walk three blocks south on Dearborn (toward Jackson St). At the corner of Dearborn & Jackson take the 151 bus toward Devon & Clark. Get off at Michigan & Huron (The Allerton will be on the right).
  • Take the Blue line to Clark and Lake. A taxi ride from Clark and Lake to The Allerton Hotel costs about $10.
  • Take a taxi directly from O'Hare to The Allerton Hotel. $35-40 if outside of rush hour.

See and Do

Chicago is a large city.

  • Visit the Art Institute of Chicago and see Edward Hopper's Nighthawks and Grant Wood's American Gothic and other notable works.
  • Follow the recommendations of others: New York Times, Lonely Planet, Chicago Tourism site
